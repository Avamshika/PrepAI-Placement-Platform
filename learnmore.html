One Platform for Complete Placement Preparation

PrepAI is an AI-powered placement preparation platform that creates a personalized roadmap for every student based on their college, branch, semester, skills, and dream company.

Why PrepAI?

Students waste hundreds of hours switching between YouTube, LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks, notes, interview experiences, resume builders, and coding platforms. PrepAI combines everything into one intelligent platform.

What You'll Get

🤖 AI Roadmap

Receive a customized daily roadmap generated specifically for your placement goals.

💻 DSA Tracker

Track solved problems, maintain streaks, and identify weak topics automatically.

📄 Resume Analyzer

Upload your resume and receive an AI score along with improvement suggestions.

🎤 AI Mock Interview

Practice technical and HR interviews with AI before attending real interviews.

📚 Smart Notes

Generate topic-wise notes, MCQs, and revision material instantly.

📈 Progress Dashboard

Visualize your preparation journey with graphs, achievements, and milestones.

How It Works

  1. Fill your academic details.
  2. Select your dream company.
  3. AI creates a personalized roadmap.
  4. Practice daily DSA and aptitude.
  5. Improve your resume.
  6. Attend AI mock interviews.
  7. Track your progress until placement.

Our Vision

Our mission is to help every engineering student prepare smarter—not harder. Instead of searching across multiple websites, PrepAI brings everything together in one place using Artificial Intelligence.
